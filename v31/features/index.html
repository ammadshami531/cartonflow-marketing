AIS satellite positions, updated in real time

The live map pulls AIS (Automatic Identification System) satellite data to show you your vessel's exact position, speed, heading, and ETA to destination port. Updated every few minutes.

  • 🛰️ Real-time AIS satellite vessel positions on an interactive world map
  • ⏱️ Port ETA predictions — updated as the vessel progresses
  • 🔖 Status chips: In Transit · Last Mile · Customs · Hold · Delayed · Pending
  • 📡 Vessel details: name, IMO, flag, speed, draft
  • ⚠️ Automatic delay alerts — flagged before they become a crisis

⚠️ The Amazon delivery window problem

Amazon FBA delivery windows are 1 week. You can only change them until 24 hours before the window opens. If your 3PL tells you a shipment is late after that deadline — you're penalized, and your ability to inbound inventory can be restricted. Live ETAs give you the information you need, early enough to act.

How it works

1
Attach vessel info

Your 3PL or you adds the vessel name/IMO to a shipment. CartonFlow links it to live AIS data automatically.

2
Watch it move

The map updates in real time. Zoom in on your vessel, see current position, speed, and trajectory to destination port.

3
Get alerts early

If ETA shifts or the vessel enters a delay status, CartonFlow alerts you — before the Amazon deadline window closes.

Competitive RFQ
Bidding System

Amazon fees are rising. E-commerce margins are under pressure. Freight is one of the few costs you can actually control — if you put your 3PLs in competition.

Start saving → Pro plan only · 14-day free trial

Post once. 3PLs compete. You save.

You create an RFQ for a shipment, select which 3PLs to invite, and set a bid deadline. Each 3PL submits DDP rate bids per service combo (e.g. Matson CLX + UPS Ground). You compare side-by-side and award in one click. No phone calls, no ambiguous email quotes.

  • 📋 Structured bids per service combo — apples-to-apples comparison
  • ⚖️ Side-by-side rate, transit days, and total cost comparison
  • 🏆 One-click award — 3PL is notified instantly
  • 📈 Bid history and awarded rate tracking per shipment
  • ⏰ 3PLs typically respond within 24 hours
  • 💰 Average 8–15% freight reduction per cycle

💰 The math

If you're spending $50K/year on freight and save 10% via competitive bidding, that's $5,000 saved — on a $129/mo platform. Most customers recoup the annual cost in a single RFQ cycle.

How it works

1
Create RFQ

Select a shipment from your pending queue. Choose service combos to bid on and set a bid deadline.

2
3PLs bid

Your invited 3PLs receive a notification, log into their portal, and submit DDP rate bids. They typically respond within 24 hours.

3
You award

Compare bids side-by-side. Click Award on the winner. The 3PL is notified and the shipment is assigned. Done.
